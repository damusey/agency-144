## The Traditional Agency Model is Broken

If you are a founder (whether you are building a small SaaS tool for local Indian businesses or a massive global B2B platform), the moment you decide to outsource your MVP, you face a dangerous crossroads. 

The traditional path is to hire a standard "Custom Software Development Agency." 

**Here is how that traditional engagement usually plays out:**
1. They quote you a 6-month timeline and a massive budget.
2. They assign a Junior Project Manager who acts as a bottleneck between you and the developers.
3. They use outdated tech stacks (like standard React + Express.js) because that is what their offshore developers learned 5 years ago.
4. When you inevitably need to pivot your product strategy in Month 3 based on early user feedback, they hit you with massive "Change Order" fees.

This model is fundamentally incompatible with the speed at which a modern startup needs to move. In 2026, the traditional agency model is dead. 

To survive, startups are partnering with **[AI-First Product Engineering Teams](/hire)**.

Traditional agencies treat AI as an afterthought—they just slap a ChatGPT API wrapper onto a bad dashboard. An [AI-first engineering team](/solutions/build) architects the application around the AI from Day 1, ensuring data security, minimal token costs, and zero hallucinations.

A common misconception is that elite engineering is only for Fortune 500 companies. This is false. 

Because our architecture relies on Serverless technologies (like Vercel and Supabase), the infrastructure scales infinitely *and* scales to zero.

You get Enterprise-grade stability on a startup budget.
